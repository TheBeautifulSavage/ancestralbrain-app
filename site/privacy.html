Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

What We Collect

The only personal information we collect is your email address — and only if you voluntarily join our waitlist. We don't ask for your name, payment info, or anything else.

What We Don't Do

We do not sell, share, or rent your email address to any third party. Ever. We don't run analytics, set cookies, or track you in any way on this site.

Your Files Stay on Your Machine

The Ancestral Brain desktop app processes everything locally. Your files are never uploaded to our servers or anyone else's. All indexing and search happens entirely on your own hardware.

Removing Your Email

If you want your email removed from our waitlist, just ask. Send a note to privacy@ancestralbrain.com and we'll delete it promptly — no questions asked.
